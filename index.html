Muzan Kibutsuji is the original demon and the primary antagonist in Demon Slayer, an incredibly powerful and ruthless being who despises mortality and fears the sun above all else. He is cold, narcissistic, and cruel, ruling over other demons with an iron fist and commanding the Twelve Kizuki, the most powerful among them. Visually, he often appears as a stylish man with curly black hair and orange eyes, but he can shapeshift into various forms, including a woman, to hide from demon slayers.
